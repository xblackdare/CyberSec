Past Cyber Attacks

if you've made it this far and still aren't convinced that anti-virus is important, let me tell you about times in the past that a lack of concern has cost corporations billions

NHS Cyber attack

if you somehow haven't heard of the cyber attack on the NHS, allow me to sum with the too long, didn't read version

in 2017 the NHS was subject to a ransomware attack, the term "ransomware" means, in a nutshell, all of the data that the NHS held was taken, corrupted and held ransom, essentially saying "Pay us or we will delete your data" this was easily avoidable by simply updating the computers windows operating system which, they never did, Staff were forced to revert to pen and paper and use their own mobiles after the attack affected key systems, including telephones. Hospitals and doctors' surgeries in parts of England were forced to turn away patients and cancel appointments after they were infected with the ransomware, which scrambled data on computers and demanded payments of $300 to $600 to restore access. People in affected areas were being advised to seek medical care only in emergencies.

sony cyber attack

On November 24, 2014, a hacker group which identified itself by the name "Guardians of Peace" leaked a release of confidential data from the film studio Sony Pictures. The data included personal information about Sony Pictures employees and their families, e-mails between employees, information about executive salaries at the company, copies of then-unreleased Sony films, and other information The hackers involved claim to have taken more than 100 terabytes of data from Sony, The attack was conducted using malware. This attack however, has been claimed to be of a political nature.

Yahoo Attack

The attack on Yahoo has been called "The worlds largest ever cyber attack" in which a hacker group forged cookies, data points that are stored on a website for ease of access, to easily log in as an administrator and gain access to all of yahoo's user files and gave them the ability to see every email on the yahoo server
